Prepare for the War to Come…

The age of magic returns to the world, bringing a new age of prosperity, knowledge and growth.

Humble citizens are taking up the sword and oath of battle. And relic-seeking warlords are consolidating their ranks, using the elemental forces of Earth, Air, Fire, Water, Light and Shadow to devastate and conquer the land.

This is Third Dawn: Siegecraft™.

Third Dawn: Siegecraft™ is the ultimate 2-player card game

Take Control of the Battlefield

Command your choice of the elemental forces of Earth, Air, Fire, Water, Light and Shadow in your own customized 60-card deck. Then, face your opponent across a unique battlefield, where your own territories - the seats of your power - are under assault by your opponent.

Each copy of Third Dawn: Siegecraft™ comes with every copy of every card you need to play the game.

Strategy At Its Finest

Use the power of Light to wear away their resistance with coordination, or use the force of Earth to crash through them with a wild Therion. Use your opponent’s momentum against them with Water or destroy their will to fight with Shadow. Burn down the gates with Fire, or evade them entirely with Air. How you win is in your hands.

There Are No Guarantees In War

The unique battle system in Third Dawn: Siegecraft™ is built to encourage strategy and long-term planning. Using dice to determine the value of Strength and Defense rolls encourages players to take daring chances, eke out narrow defenses and win improbable battles. Every game of Third Dawn: Siegecraft™ is unique and memorable.
