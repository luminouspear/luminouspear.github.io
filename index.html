Siege the Day

The age of magic returns to the world, bringing relic-seeking warlords, using the elemental forces of Earth, Air, Fire, Water, Light and Shadow to devastate and conquer the land.

This is Third Dawn: Siegecraft™, a hybrid card game.

Third Dawn: Siegecraft™ is the ultimate 2-player card game

Charge into Battle

Using dice, position and timing, Third Dawn: Siegecraft™ expands on the standard collectible card game, by giving you more options in the game and when building your custom decks.

Control Your Fortune

Cast powerful spells that will help you control the flow of combat, using the 6 distinct elements of Third Dawn: Siegecraft™. Learn to master unique combinations of spells and abilities to express yourself and take out your opponents.

Conquer Three Territories to Win

March your units onto your opponent's territories and lay siege. With the territory undefended, your forces are free to pillage.

Take Your Shot

Third Dawn: Siegecraft™ can be learned in as little as 15 minutes, but will take a lifetime to master. Get started today!

Master the Elements

Life Begins Anew

Using the element Earth, your empire begins from the humblest origins.

The Battlefield Crackles with Energy

Air mages specialize in the art of surprise, striking when their enemies least suspect.

The Battle Never Stops

As long as a flicker can turn into a flame, Fire is never gone.

Control the Flow of Combat

Those who rely on Water understand that time, patience and experience win battles.

Seek Shelter in Strength

Submit to the Light to gain its power, or fall to those who have.

The Inevitable End

Take on the power of Shadow and strike without warning or mercy.

Everything You Need to Play The Game

Your copy of Third Dawn: Siegecraft™ comes with everything you need to play the game.

With over 200 unique cards, two custom playmats, dice and rulebook, you'll be ready to go from the moment you get the game.
